Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how GameGou ("GameGou", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our games (including Perfect Kick, Perfect Kick 2, Top Football Manager, Soccer Strike, and any future titles), visit our websites, or otherwise interact with us. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and profile data, such as your username, display name, and basic profile information if you sign in through a platform or social network.
  • Device and usage data, such as device identifiers, operating system, language, IP address, and gameplay data (progress, purchases, session length, and events).
  • Purchase information, processed by the app store (such as Google Play or the App Store). We receive non-sensitive information about the items you purchase so we can deliver them.
  • Support communications when you contact us through email or in-game support tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our games and services.
  • Operate security features, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Personalise in-game experiences, such as matchmaking and recommended events.
  • Measure performance and understand how players interact with our games.
  • Communicate with you about updates, events, and changes to this policy.

Advertising and Analytics

Our games and websites may use third-party analytics and advertising partners that collect information through SDKs and cookies. These partners help us understand performance and may show personalised ads in accordance with their own privacy policies and your platform settings.

Children's Privacy

Our games are generally designed for a broad audience. Where required by local law, we take steps to limit data collection from younger players and to comply with regulations such as COPPA or the GDPR where applicable.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing.

To exercise these rights or make a privacy request, please contact us at privacy@gamegou.com. We may need additional information to verify your identity before completing your request.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

International Transfers

GameGou may process and store information in countries other than where you live. When we transfer data, we use appropriate safeguards required by applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will take reasonable steps to notify you, such as posting a notice in our games or on this site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at privacy@gamegou.com.
